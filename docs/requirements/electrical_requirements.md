
**TTGO T-display ESP32 specifications:**  
– Supply voltage: 3.3V DC or 5V DC  
– GPIO Voltage: 3.3V*  
– ESP32 chip (240Mhz dual core processor)  
– SRAM: 520KB  
– Flash memory: 4MB  
- Wi-Fi  
- Bluetooth  
– 1.14 inch (240×135) TFT (IPS) display (ST7789)  
– 2x push button  
– Li-ion/Li-Po battery charging circuit: TP4054 chip can charge up to 500mA
